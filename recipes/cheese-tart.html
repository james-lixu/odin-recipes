Cheese Tart

Description:

Cheese Tart is a dessert made of creamy and velvety cheese filling that is nestled on a flaky cream cheese pie crust. The tandem of the two combined makes for such a heavenly, rich dessert. Baked Cheese tart is widely popular across Asia, Australia and pretty much available across the United States and major European cities like London.

Ingredients:

Crust Ingredients:
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Salt
  • All purpose flour
  • Lemon zest
  • Egg yolk

Cheese Filling Ingredients:
  • Cream cheese
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Unsalted butter
  • Milk
  • Powdered sugar
  • Corn starch
  • Lemon juice
  • Vanilla extract
  • Egg

Cooking Steps:

  1. Make the tart crusts.
  2. Make the cheese filling.
  3. Bake the tart shells.
  4. Fill the tart shells.
  5. Bake again and broil for the last couple minutes until golden brown.
