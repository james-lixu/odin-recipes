Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a very high temperature.

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • All purpose flour
  • Oul
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Cooking Steps:

  1. Make the dough. Knead it and let it rise.
  2. Punch and shape the dough into desired form.
  3. Add your toppings and bake at your oven's highest setting.
